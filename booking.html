Deposit & Pricing

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. This deposit is applied toward the total cost of your tattoo. Pricing varies based on complexity, size, and placement — discussed during consultation.

What to Expect

  • Initial consultation via email or in-person
  • Custom design created specifically for you
  • Design reveal before your appointment
  • Flexible scheduling for multi-session pieces
  • Detailed aftercare instructions provided

Booking Policy

I work exclusively on custom pieces — no flash, no walk-ins. Please allow 1–2 weeks for a response. Rescheduling requires 48-hour notice. No-shows forfeit the deposit.

Currently Booking

Accepting select clients for large-scale custom work. Wait times vary depending on project scope. The sooner you reach out, the sooner we begin.
